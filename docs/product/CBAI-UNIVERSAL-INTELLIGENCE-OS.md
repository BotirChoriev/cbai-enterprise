## What CBAI is

CBAI is a **Universal Intelligence Operating System for Humanity** — not a website, dashboard, chatbot, or generic AI platform.

The home surface is the **Intelligence Canvas**: a living mission workspace where every visible element answers:

- What is happening now?
- What should happen next?
- What knowledge is missing?
- What evidence is required?
- What impact will this create?

---

## Who it connects

Scientists, researchers, independent thinkers, students, academics, universities, laboratories, governments, investors, companies, engineers, doctors, and citizens — inside one evidence-first, mission-driven environment.

---

## Core operating objects

| Object | Role |
|--------|------|
| Mission | Primary organizing problem |
| Question | Framed inquiry linked to projects |
| Evidence | Traceable references — never fabricated |
| Knowledge | Missing knowledge and streams |
| Impact | Humanity impact review |
| Operator | Persistent assistant — intervenes only when valuable |
| Knowledge Universe | Mission-aware graph of real entities |

---

## What CBAI is not

- Not ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Notion, Linear, or Cursor
- Not a marketing homepage or admin panel
- Not a popularity or ranking platform
- Not a profession-only portal system
